## Key Heart Concepts

### The Heart

A nightmare dungeon beneath the mile-high city of Spire, filled with:

- Strange landmarks that defy reality
- Dangerous domains with unique hazards
- Treasures and horrors in equal measure
- The promise of your deepest desires

### Stress & Fallout

- Characters accumulate stress across five resistances
- When stress fills, you suffer fallout
- Fallout ranges from minor setbacks to character death
- The deeper you go, the worse it gets

### Delving

- Each delve is a journey into the unknown
- Navigate through interconnected domains
- Discover landmarks that change the rules
- Balance risk and reward
