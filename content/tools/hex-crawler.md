## Features

- **Hex Map Generation** - Create wilderness hex maps
- **Terrain Types** - Forests, mountains, plains, swamps, and more
- **Point of Interest** - Generate locations and encounters
- **Travel Tracking** - Monitor party movement across hexes
- **Random Encounters** - Generate events during travel
- **Export Maps** - Save and share your hex crawls

## How to Use

1. **Generate Map** - Create a hex-based wilderness area
2. **Set Terrain** - Define terrain types for each hex
3. **Add Locations** - Place settlements, dungeons, and landmarks
4. **Track Travel** - Monitor party position and movement
5. **Roll Encounters** - Generate random events
6. **Explore** - Reveal hexes as players discover them

## Hex Crawl Concepts

### Exploration
- Each hex represents a region to explore
- Travel takes time and resources
- Discovering new hexes reveals content
- Map fills in as players explore

### Encounters
- Random encounters during travel
- Planned locations in specific hexes
- Environmental challenges
- Resource management

### Scale
- Adjust hex size for your campaign
- 6-mile hexes (classic D&D)
- 12-mile hexes (larger scale)
- Custom sizes for your needs

## Tips for GMs

- Prepare key locations in advance
- Use random generation for filler
- Track weather and seasons
- Consider travel time and supplies
- Reward exploration with discoveries
- Connect hexes with roads and rivers

## System Agnostic

Works with any tabletop RPG:
- D&D and Pathfinder
- OSR games
- Shadowrun (wilderness runs)
- Any game with overland travel
