## Features

- **Edition Toggle** - Switch between 2nd and 3rd Edition rules
- **Automatic Initiative Passes** - Handles multiple passes correctly
- **Condition Monitors** - Track physical and stun damage
- **Damage Integration** - Initiative automatically reduces as characters take damage
- **Add/Remove Combatants** - Manage NPCs and PCs on the fly
- **Initiative Rolls** - Roll for all combatants or enter manually

## How to Use

1. **Select Edition** - Choose SR2 or SR3 rules
2. **Add Combatants** - Enter names and initiative scores
3. **Roll Initiative** - Let the tracker roll or enter values manually
4. **Track Combat** - Click through initiative passes
5. **Apply Damage** - Update condition monitors as needed

## Edition Differences

### 2nd Edition
- Base 10 initiative system
- Initiative passes at 10-second intervals

### 3rd Edition  
- Base 10 initiative system
- Multiple initiative passes based on initiative score
- Automatic initiative reduction with damage
