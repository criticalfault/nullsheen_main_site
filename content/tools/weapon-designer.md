## Features

- **Custom Weapon Creation** - Design firearms, melee, and exotic weapons
- **Balanced Stats** - Automatic cost calculation based on stats
- **Modification System** - Add accessories and modifications
- **Damage Calculation** - Power, staging, and recoil values
- **Availability Rating** - Determine street availability
- **Export Options** - Save and share your designs

## How to Use

1. **Choose Weapon Type** - Firearm, melee, or exotic
2. **Set Base Stats** - Damage, range, and rate of fire
3. **Add Modifications** - Scopes, silencers, smartlinks, etc.
4. **Calculate Cost** - Tool balances price automatically
5. **Review Stats** - Check concealability and availability
6. **Export** - Save for use in your game

## Design Tips

- Start with a concept (corp security, street gang, etc.)
- Balance power with availability
- Consider concealability for runners
- Add modifications that fit the theme
- Price should reflect rarity and power

## SR3 Weapon Stats

The designer handles:
- **Damage Code** - Power and staging
- **Concealability** - How easy to hide
- **Recoil** - Penalty per shot
- **Rate of Fire** - SS, SA, BF, FA
- **Ammo Capacity** - Magazine size
- **Availability** - Street index and legality
