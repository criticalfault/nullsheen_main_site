## Features

- **Priority System** - Classic Shadowrun character creation
- **2nd & 3rd Edition Support** - Toggle between editions
- **Automatic Calculations** - Karma costs, skill points, and attribute limits
- **Equipment Selection** - Browse and purchase gear, weapons, and cyberware
- **Character Export** - Save and print your character sheets
- **Beta Version** - Actively being developed with new features

## How to Use

1. **Choose Your Edition** - Select Shadowrun 2nd or 3rd Edition
2. **Set Priorities** - Assign priorities to Race, Magic, Attributes, Skills, and Resources
3. **Build Your Character** - Allocate points and select equipment
4. **Review & Export** - Check your character and export when ready

## Supported Editions

- **Shadowrun 2nd Edition** - Full priority system support
- **Shadowrun 3rd Edition** - Complete character creation rules

## Tips for New Players

- Start with a concept (street samurai, decker, mage, etc.)
- Balance combat and non-combat skills
- Don't forget to buy a commlink and basic gear
- Save frequently during character creation
