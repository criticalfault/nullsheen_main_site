## Features

- **Status Effect Tracking** - Monitor all active conditions
- **Radiation Exposure** - Track rads and radiation sickness
- **Injury Management** - Record wounds and their effects
- **Combat Tracking** - Initiative and action point management
- **Condition Monitoring** - Health, hunger, thirst, and fatigue
- **Party Management** - Track multiple characters

## How to Use

1. **Add Characters** - Create entries for PCs and NPCs
2. **Track Status** - Apply conditions and effects
3. **Monitor Radiation** - Record rad exposure and effects
4. **Manage Combat** - Track initiative and actions
5. **Update Conditions** - Modify as situations change
6. **Clear Effects** - Remove conditions when resolved

## What is Fallout 2d20?

The Fallout RPG uses the Modiphius 2d20 system, featuring:
- Roll 2d20 against target numbers
- Action points for combat actions
- Radiation as a constant threat
- Injuries and conditions that matter
- Wasteland survival mechanics

## Perfect For

- **Fallout GMs** - Keep track of complex status effects

## Status Effects Include

- Radiation sickness levels

## Tips for GMs

- Update radiation after each exposure
- Track injuries for realistic consequences
- Use conditions to add tension
- Monitor party resources (stimpaks, rad-away)
- Apply environmental effects consistently
